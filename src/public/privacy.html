Pulso WhatsApp

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Pulso uses WhatsApp and internal systems to communicate with care providers, collect onboarding details, review submitted documents, and coordinate service operations.

What we collect

  • Name, phone number, and profile information shared through WhatsApp.
  • Qualification details such as GDA, GNM, or ANM status.
  • Documents uploaded during onboarding, including CVs and certificates.
  • Conversation history needed to continue support and onboarding.

How we use the data

  • To respond to WhatsApp inquiries and automate onboarding messages.
  • To verify qualifications and supporting documents.
  • To manage provider onboarding, follow-up, and operations review.
  • To improve service quality, reliability, and compliance.

How data is stored

Data may be stored in Pulso-managed systems including secure cloud databases, hosting platforms, and media storage used for operations and onboarding workflows.

Sharing

Pulso does not sell personal information. Data is shared only with service providers or internal reviewers when required to operate the onboarding workflow, deliver support, or comply with legal obligations.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your onboarding data by contacting Pulso using the support channels listed below.
