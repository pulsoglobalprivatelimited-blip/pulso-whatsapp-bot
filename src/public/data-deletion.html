Pulso WhatsApp

Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 24, 2026

If you want Pulso to delete your WhatsApp onboarding data, follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to galexys4r@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include the phone number used to contact Pulso on WhatsApp.
  4. Include any additional information needed to identify your records.

What happens next

Pulso will review the request and delete or anonymize eligible records within a reasonable period, subject to legal, operational, and compliance requirements.

Questions

For questions about deletion requests, contact galexys4r@gmail.com.
